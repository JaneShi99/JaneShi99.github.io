About me

I am a second year math PhD student at MIT, very fortunate to be advised by Drew Sutherland.

I am interested in computational number theory. In particular, I am interested in computing the L-functions of abelian surfaces.

Previously, I was a pure math masters student at the University of Cambridge with a focus in number theory.

Even before, I was an undergraduate student at the University of Waterloo studying a double major in CS and Pure Math. I was fortunate to be advised by David Jao. I worked on elliptic curve isogeny-based cryptography.
